Get paid online in 3 steps.

Billplz is Malaysia's cheapest payment gateway for F&B. No monthly fee, no setup fee. From RM0.75 per transaction — FPX bank transfer, DuitNow QR, Touch 'n Go, GrabPay, all in one.

  1. Sign up at billplz.com

    Free Basic plan, no credit card required. Submit your SSM registration + business bank account. Approval typically within 1–3 business days.

  2. Create a Collection

    In your Billplz dashboard, create one Collection called "Manila Tambayan Orders". This gives you a collection ID you'll use to generate payment links per order.

  3. Generate a Bill for each order

    When a WhatsApp order arrives, create a Bill in the dashboard (or via API) with the exact total and the customer's name. Billplz returns a link — paste it back into the same WhatsApp chat. Customer pays; you see it in your dashboard instantly.

  4. Funds settle next business day

    FPX payments settle to your Malaysian bank account the next business day. No waiting 7 days like PayPal. Cash flow stays healthy.

  5. Set up 3 WhatsApp Quick Replies

    In WhatsApp Business → Settings → Quick Replies, save these 3 canned messages. You'll send one of them on every single order — they take one tap instead of typing.

    Shortcut: /nopayment
