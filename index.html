For the restaurant owner

Get paid via OCBC OneCollect.

No API keys. No backend. No code to configure. When a customer sends their WhatsApp order, you open the OCBC OneCollect app, enter the total, and send them a QR code to scan. Payment lands in your OCBC account instantly.

  1. Open an OCBC Business Account

    You need an existing OCBC conventional or Islamic current account. Apply online at ocbc.com.my if you don't have one yet. This is the account your payments settle into.

  2. Download OCBC OneCollect Malaysia

    Search "OCBC OneCollect Malaysia" on Google Play or the App Store — make sure it has the blue tick. Register with your business mobile number, set a 6-digit PIN, and submit your account details. Approval takes up to 7 working days.

  3. Generate a QR for each order

    When an order arrives on WhatsApp, open OCBC OneCollect, enter the exact total in MYR, and tap Generate QR. A Dynamic DuitNow QR appears with the amount pre-filled — the customer cannot pay the wrong amount. Screenshot it and send the image back to the customer in the same WhatsApp chat.

  4. Customer pays — you get an instant push notification

    The customer saves your QR image, opens their banking app, taps Scan QR → Import from Gallery, selects the image, and confirms. You receive a push notification and audible alert on the OCBC OneCollect app the moment payment is confirmed — no need to check manually. Singapore customers can request a PayNow QR instead and pay in SGD from their Singapore bank; you receive the equivalent in MYR. Only start cooking after the notification arrives.

  5. Set up 3 WhatsApp Quick Replies

    In WhatsApp Business → Settings → Quick Replies, save these 3 canned messages. You'll send one on every single order — they take one tap instead of typing.

    Shortcut: /confirm
    ✅ Order received! Please scan the QR code below to pay. We'll start preparing as soon as payment is confirmed. Thank you!
    Shortcut: /ready
    🍱 Your order is ready for pickup at our counter! Please come collect at your earliest convenience. Thank you for ordering from Kabayan Tambayan!
    Shortcut: /nopayment
    ⚠️ Hi! We haven't received payment confirmation yet. Could you please scan the QR code we sent and complete the payment? We'll start preparing right away once confirmed. Thank you!
